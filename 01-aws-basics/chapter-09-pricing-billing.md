# Module 1, Chapter 9: Pricing, Billing, and Support

## 🧠 Part 1: Deep Theory (How AWS Makes Money)
1.  **Pay-as-you-go**: No long-term contracts. You pay only for what you use.
2.  **Savings Plans / Reserved Instances**: Commit to 1 or 3 years to save up to 72%.
3.  **Consolidated Billing**: If you have multiple accounts, you get one bill and volume discounts.
4.  **AWS Support Plans**:
    * **Basic**: Free. No tech support.
    * **Developer**: $29/mo. Email support (24hr).
    * **Business**: $100/mo. 24/7 Phone/Chat/Email support.
    * **Enterprise**: $15k/mo. Includes a Technical Account Manager (TAM).

## 🏗️ Part 2: 10 Master Scenario-Based Analysis

| # | Scenario | Correct Tool / Plan | Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | You want to estimate the cost of a new project before you build it. | **AWS Pricing Calculator** | Estimate costs for any AWS service. |
| 2 | You want to see a graph of your spending for the last 6 months. | **AWS Cost Explorer** | Visualize and analyze your costs over time. |
| 3 | You want to receive an email the moment your bill hits €50. | **AWS Budgets** | Set custom budgets and receive alerts. |
| 4 | Your company has 5 departments with 5 AWS accounts; you want one bill. | **AWS Organizations** | Enables Consolidated Billing for all accounts. |
| 5 | You are a big bank and need a dedicated person (TAM) to help you. | **Enterprise Support Plan** | Only Enterprise includes a dedicated TAM. |
| 6 | You want to find out which tags (labels) are costing you the most money. | **Cost Allocation Tags** | Track costs by specific labels or departments. |
| 7 | You want to see exactly which hour of the day was the most expensive. | **Cost & Usage Report** | The most detailed billing data available. |
| 8 | You are a small developer and just need email support for a bug. | **Developer Support Plan** | Lowest cost plan with technical support. |
| 9 | You want to download a CSV of your monthly spending. | **AWS Billing Dashboard** | Main hub for invoices and payment data. |
| 10 | You want to pay a lower price because you use 500TB of storage. | **Volume Discounts** | AWS lowers unit costs as your usage increases. |

## 🎙️ Part 3: Top 10 Priority Interview Q&A

| # | Question | Answer |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **What is the AWS Pricing Calculator?** | A web tool to estimate costs for any AWS service. |
| 2 | **What is the benefit of Consolidated Billing?** | One bill for multiple accounts and shared volume discounts. |
| 3 | **What is a TAM?** | Technical Account Manager (Available only on Enterprise support). |
| 4 | **Does a Budget alert stop your services?** | No, it only sends a notification. |
| 5 | **Which Support Plan includes Trusted Advisor?** | Business and Enterprise include the full version. |
| 6 | **What is the AWS Free Tier?** | Offers for new customers to try services at no cost. |
| 7 | **What are Cost Allocation Tags?** | Labels used to track and organize costs. |
| 8 | **What is the AWS Marketplace?** | A catalog to buy 3rd-party software running on AWS. |
| 9 | **Cost of Basic Support?** | $0 (Included for everyone). |
| 10 | **Business Support response time?** | Under 1 hour for urgent cases. |
